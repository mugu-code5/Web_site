About IITM

Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM) is one of the premier engineering institutions in India. Established in 1959, IITM offers world-class education and research facilities in various fields of engineering, science, and technology.

  • Department of Computer Science and Engineering
  • Department of Electrical Engineering
  • Department of Mechanical Engineering
